\subsection{Score function estimator}

(This tutorial follows the
\href{tut_KLqp.html}{$\text{KL}(q\|p)$ minimization} tutorial.)

We seek to maximize the ELBO,
\begin{align*}
  \lambda^*
  &=
  \arg \max_\lambda \text{ELBO}(\lambda)\\
  &=\;
  \arg \max_\lambda\;
  \mathbb{E}_{q(z\;;\;\lambda)}
  \big[
  \log p(x, z)
  -
  \log q(z\;;\;\lambda)
  \big].
\end{align*}
